How to get there

Most popular airlines to fly from London to Iceland are Icelander and British Airways.

Moreover, the average price for return flights is around £250, but cheaper airlines such as Ryanair offer flights starting from £72.


Weather

Temperature

  • Iceland in the contrary to popular belief has a moderate climate
  • Average temperature during the summer is between 10-13°C
  • Average temperature during the winter is about 0°C

When to visit?

Icelandic winds can be strong which can disrupt your travel. The best time to visit might be during the summer as the winter storms are frequent. However, the cheapest time is from January to May.


Top 5 places to visit

  1. Skaftafell ice cave
  2. Blue lagoon - geothermal spa
  3. Golden circle tourist route
  4. Reykjavik - capital city

Food recommondations

Icelandic food is among the healthiest in the world. The crystal clear water and air, the freely grazing sheep and cows, the wild fish and the chemical-free plants make excellent ingredients for what may be the best quality food you have ever tried.

  • Hakarl - Fermented shark meat
  • Hardfiskur - Dried fish
  • Rugbraud - Dark rye bread from a hot spring

Useful phrases

While Icelandic is the official language, English is taught as a second language, so almost every Icelander speaks the language fluently.

  • Hello - Halló
  • Thank you! - Takk / Takk fyrir
  • Yes / No - Já / Nei
  • Goodbye - Bless
